Gavel Privacy Policy

Effective date: [DATE PAGE GOES LIVE]

Gavel is a bedtime alarm app made by an independent developer. This policy describes what data the app handles. The short version: there are no accounts, your personal data stays on your phone, and we collect only anonymous usage analytics.

Data that stays on your device

Your bedtime, settings, quiz answers from setup, and your night-by-night streak are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

Data we collect

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Mixpanel to record anonymous events about how the app is used — for example, that an alarm fired or a setup step was completed. This is tied to a random device identifier, not to your name, email, or any account (none exist). We use it solely to understand and improve the app. It is never used to track you across other apps or websites, and it is never sold or shared for advertising.

Purchase status. Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription status; RevenueCat receives Apple's purchase receipt and a random anonymous identifier. We never see your payment details.

What we don't do

We don't collect names, emails, contacts, locations, photos, or health data. We don't show ads. We don't sell data. We don't use cross-app tracking of any kind.

Your choices

You can stop all data collection by deleting the app. Subscription management and refunds are handled through your Apple account settings.
